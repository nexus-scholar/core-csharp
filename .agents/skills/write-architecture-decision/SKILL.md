---
name: write-architecture-decision
description: Create or revise a Nexus ADR when behavior sources conflict or a durable architecture, compatibility, privacy, or security choice is required.
---

Write the ADR under `docs/adr/` with status, date, context, decision, alternatives, consequences, migration effect, fixture effect, and reversal conditions.

Cite the specification, PHP behavior, tests, and operational constraints that motivate the decision. Distinguish present requirements from future options.

Do not use an ADR to hide an unresolved scientific-method question. Name the decision owner when human methodological judgment is required.
